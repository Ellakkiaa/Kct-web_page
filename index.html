Kumaraguru Hostels:Kumaraguru students Hostel is an integral part of the college campus. It has two distinct sections for boys’ and girls’ students. The boys hostel comprises of six blocks with total of 512 rooms with a capacity to accommodate 2178 students. The girls’ hostel has a total of 264 rooms in its three blocks with a capacity to accommodate 1216 students. Apart from the hostel blocks, the hostel has dormitories in Vallalar Maiyam and Sharada Maiyam that can accommodate around 90 and 70 students respectively. The residents of the hostel are given rooms in a sharing basis. All common areas like dining, washrooms, lobby etc. are provided in the hostels. Every hostel block has solar hot water facility, mineral water, Hot/Cold water dispenser, Newspaper, Wi-Fi and common ironing and reading room. TV, Hub (Browsing Centre), KRAFT (Gym) and indoor games are available as common facility for the hostel students. Dining hall is conveniently located close to the hostels. Hive offers a premier off-campus residential facility for Kumaraguru boy student residents located in the close proximity to the campus. Hive is a five storeyed residential space with modern amenities houses 58 spacious rooms.
